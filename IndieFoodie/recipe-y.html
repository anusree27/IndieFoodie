Yakhni Biriyani

If you thought Biryani’s had to be super rich and loaded with spice, The Yakhni Biryani is bound to surprise. Mildly flavoured, this is a winter favourite.

Yakhni Biryani or yakhni pulao. Yakhni biryani is especially popular in North India and tastes amazingly delicious. As rice are cooked along with juice of spices and chicken, you will feel the taste of biryani in every rice grain. It’s very easy to prepare restaurant style muradabadi yakhni chicken biryani. So, let’s see how to prepare this easy, yummy and spicy yakhni chicken biryani recipe at home.
